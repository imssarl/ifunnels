About Zeal PreSchool

Zeal Montessori is headquartered in Uttarahalli, Bangalore with the best teachers to educate your kids. At Zeal we believe education is the cornerstone of a successful and fulfilling life.

At Zeal, we also understand that the environment plays a vital role in developing a happy, confident, secure and competent child with an inquiring mind. We therefore lay great emphasis on creating a carefully planned and well-resourced environment that is warm, welcoming and engaging.
